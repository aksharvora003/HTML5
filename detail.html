GUJARAT

Gujarat is a state located on the western coast of India, known for its rich history, diverse culture, and thriving economy. Here are some key points about Gujarat: - **Capital**: Gandhinagar (administrative), Ahmedabad (commercial and cultural hub) - **Language**: Gujarati (primary), Hindi - **Population**: Over 60 million - **Geography**: Gujarat is bordered by Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, and the Arabian Sea. - **Economy**: One of India’s most industrialized states, with strengths in petrochemicals, textiles, and agriculture. It is a major producer of salt, cotton, and groundnut. - **Culture**: Known for vibrant festivals like Navratri, and for its traditional dance forms such as Garba and Dandiya. Gujarat has a rich heritage in art, architecture, and cuisine. - **Famous People**: Mahatma Gandhi, the leader of India’s independence movement, was from Gujarat, as well as other influential figures like Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel. - **Tourist Attractions**: The Rann of Kutch, Gir National Park (home to Asiatic lions), Somnath Temple, and the historic city of Ahmedabad (a UNESCO World Heritage Site). Gujarat plays a significant role in India's cultural and economic landscape.
